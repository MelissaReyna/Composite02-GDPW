About Garavito's

Home > About me

Garavito is a hip-hop artist who started around 2012 influenced by electronic sounds and lately by hip hop music. Born in Monterrey, Mexico, in 1999, Garavito grew up listening to a wide range of music, from rock to electronic music, but he was drawn to hip-hop as a form of expression that spoke to his experiences growing up.

He started recording and performing his own rhythms as a teenager, and quickly gained a following for his gritty, honest lyrics and powerful stage presence.
